Transparency Leaders

Manhattan and Brooklyn CBs show the highest transparency scores, with consistent posting of meeting materials and active social media engagement. These boards serve as models for best practices.

Resource Disparities

Significant variation in digital capacity correlates with board funding and staff support. Boards in under-resourced districts struggle with website maintenance and document accessibility.
